Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Quick Registration ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our registration service.

2. Information We Collect

Our service is designed to be privacy-friendly:

  • Direct Communications: When you click our email or WhatsApp buttons, your communication goes directly to the specified recipient through your own email client or WhatsApp application. We do not store or process this communication on our servers.
  • No Account Creation: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use our service.
  • Technical Information: Like most websites, we may collect anonymous technical data such as IP address, browser type, and operating system for security and analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use minimal technical information to:

  • Ensure the security and proper functioning of our website
  • Improve user experience
  • Monitor website performance

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use a simple cookie to remember your cookie preferences. No tracking cookies or third-party analytics are used by default.

5. Third-Party Services

Our service includes links to third-party platforms:

  • Email Clients: When you click the email button, you are redirected to your default email application.
  • WhatsApp: When you click the WhatsApp button, you are redirected to WhatsApp Web or the WhatsApp application.
  • Font Libraries: We use Font Awesome and Google Fonts for icons and typography.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

6. Data Security

Since we do not store personal data on our servers, there is minimal risk of data breach. Our website uses HTTPS encryption to protect communications between your browser and our server.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. Since we collect minimal data, your rights include:

  • The right to access what little data might be collected
  • The right to withdraw cookie consent
  • The right to complain to a data protection authority

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us through the email or WhatsApp buttons on our main page.
